About IBG

Imronbek Business Group - Your partner in global business solutions

IBG – Imronbek Business Group

IBG is a diversified holding company headquartered in Tashkent, Uzbekistan. Our core focus is logistics and freight solutions, providing reliable and transparent supply chain services for regional and international partners.

In addition, IBG operates through specialized subsidiaries in investment management and global trade, enabling long-term value creation and cross-border business growth.

With a global mindset, we deliver comprehensive logistics, trade, and investment solutions from Uzbekistan to international markets.
